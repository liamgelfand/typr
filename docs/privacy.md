# Typr Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-28

Typr is a **local-first** typing coach. Your keystroke data stays on your device unless you explicitly export it.

## What we store locally

- Keystroke events (key code, timestamp, hashed window title)
- Inferred typing corrections and bigram statistics
- Practice session results and spaced-repetition drill cards
- Your settings and app blocklist patterns

## What we never store

- Raw window titles (only a one-way hash for context grouping)
- Keystrokes from blocklisted apps (password managers, terminals, etc.)
- Cloud uploads of keystroke data (no Typr server receives your typing)

## What you control

- **Pause / stop capture** at any time via the tray menu or in-app controls
- **App blocklist** — add patterns to exclude sensitive applications
- **Export** — download a JSON snapshot of your aggregated stats
- **Delete all data** — wipe the local database from Settings

## Optional telemetry

If you opt in (default: **off**), Typr may send crash reports via Sentry containing:

- App version and OS
- Stack traces

Crash reports **never** include keystroke content or database contents.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue in the project repository.
